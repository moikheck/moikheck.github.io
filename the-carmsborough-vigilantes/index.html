The Carmsborough Vigilantes Series

Release Order Chronological Order

"Beggars can't be choosers, But I'm done begging." Follow the story of Luna, a homeless teen with skewed morals, as she and her mechanical cat Freckles struggle to live a normal, problem-free life that keeps getting interrupted by bad men with bad plans. Read #lunaontherun completely free online.

Sometime after the events of Luna on the Run, Luna and her mechanical cat Freckles have taken up the role of vigilantism in Carmsborough, looking to rid it of the mob boss Bijabers' influence. Despite her noble cause, her unconventional ways and distressed morals bring her many opponents, like S6, a secret American government organization, and the other vigilante duo of Carmsborough: Orion and the Clockwork. Now Luna must race the clock and remain anonymous while cleaning up the streets of Carmsborough under her threatening new alias: The Blood Moon Pirate.

Orion has been chosen to be protected by the Clockwork, a seemingly indestructible metal golem designed to guard him at all costs.

He did not sign up for this.

Add to the fact that a man named Syndra has taken over the country and wants to kill him and steal the Clockwork, and his relationship with Rami, a boy from the countryside, is cut abruptly short because of it, and Orion finds himself wishing the mysterious machine had never chosen him to begin with.

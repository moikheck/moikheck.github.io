Summary

Freckles is Luna Wells' steambot companion during the events of the books in the Carmsborough Vigilantes series. Freckles cannot talk, and is instead limited to what other people interpret from his meowing.

Creation

Freckles is a steambot created by the Gearmaster, a skilled engineer that created all of the steambots. Freckles is the only cat steambot.



Luna on the Run

In Luna on the Run, Freckles is separated from Luna, his human companion, and must be rescued from Slaphand, a notorious crime boss in Carmsborough. He is placed in a cage and freed at the end of the book. Luna interprets his meowing as a voice of reason, acting as her conscience.



The Blood Moon Pirate

In The Blood Moon Pirate, Freckles joins Luna on her quests to take down different members of Bijabers' entourage. He even actively hunts and takes down one of them, Jack Aaron, at the zoo on their day off. However, his moral voice of reason is often shoved to the side in favor of Luna's bloodlust.



The Gearmaster's Apprentice

Freckles is set to make an appearance in The Gearmaster's Apprentice, the third full-length book of the Carmsborough Vigilantes series.

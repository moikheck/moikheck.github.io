Summary

Orion is a teenager who lives in the Housing District of Carmsborough. He lives with father and sister, Sam, in an apartment complex. Upon receiving the Clockwork during an event held by the Gearmaster, Orion is hunted by Syndra, an ex-military man attempting a coup, and flees to the Lower Agricultural District. There he meets Rami, his love interest, and learns more about the Clockwork.

Early Life

When Orion was young, his mother was diagnosed with cancer, and passed away a few months later. Paying off the hospital bills put his dad in extreme debt, something they've had to deal with for years after.

As a teen, Orion was taking classes to become a cloudship pilot, but after nearly failing, he had to drop out to help his father pay back their debt. Instead, he continued with his cello lessons.



Orion and the Clockwork

Orion wakes to find out it's the day that the Gearmaster will present his latest steambot, the Clockwork. Orion heads to his cello practice, then realizes it's closed for the day, so stops into a diner he's never noticed before.

After spending some time with the diner owner, he goes to the town square for the event, which is interrupted by Syndra and his men, who are after the Clockwork. Syndra shoots the Gearmaster and takes the remote for the Clockwork, which activates and chooses Orion as its person to protect.

From there, Orion is chased through the Housing District as the Clockwork protects him, then takes him to the Lower Agricultural District in a crash landing. Once awake, he finds Rami and his sister, Max, who convince their parents to let Orion stay.

After experimenting and learning how the Clockwork works, Max is taken to the hospital for a broken leg, and Orion develops feelings for Rami, which lead up to a kiss spotted by Rami's mom.

His mom reports Orion's location to Syndra, who comes to take the Clockwork, forcing Orion to flee.

Afterwards, he rescues the Gearmaster and fights Syndra and his men, saving Carmsborough from their grasp.

At the end, Orion is approached by Lukas, who offers to take the Syndra Stone Orion found for a massive lump of money.



Luna on the Run

While Orion is not present for most of Luna on the Run, he appears briefly to respond to a call about a fight breaking out in an apartment complex, started by Luna Wells. He tries to find her at the end of the big chase scene, but does not get her.



The Blood Moon Pirate

Orion is more active during the events of The Blood Moon Pirate, where he repeatedly attempts to stop Luna from killing some of Bijabers' men. This culminates in the last chapter, where they go head-to-head, and Orion ends up defeated by the vigilante in America.



The Gearmaster's Apprentice

Orion is set to make an appearance in The Gearmaster's Apprentice, the third full-length book of the Carmsborough Vigilantes series.

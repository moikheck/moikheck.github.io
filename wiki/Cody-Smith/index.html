Summary

Cody is a highschooler who was on a trip to Egypt with his classmates when their plane was taken down by a mysterious attack. He is the forced to survive through the wreckage of the Hindenburg, which had previously been missing, with his girlfriend, Holly McCay. He ends up dying inside.

Early Life

Not much is known about Cody's early life, but it is known that he has been in a relationship with Holly McCay for a considerable amount of time before the events of The Ghost of the Hindenburg.



The Ghost of the Hindenburg

In The Ghost of the Hindenburg, Cody survives a plane crash that lands her and her classmates at the wreckage of the Hindenburg. He attempts to survive the haunted interior of the lost airship along with his classmates Tes Simms, Stacy, his girlfriend Holly, and Wesley. He ends up being killed at the hands of The Smoker.

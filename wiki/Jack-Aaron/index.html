Summary

Jack Aaron is one of Bijabers' crime boss leaders. He was target of Freckles in The Blood Moon Pirate, where he was arrested by Carmsborough police in the zoo.

Work for Bijabers

Jack Aaron's role is not made explicitly clear in The Blood Moon Pirate, but it is known that he works for Bijabers. It is revealed when arrested that he has charges against him for twelve murders, money laundering, war crimes, and tax evasion.



The Blood Moon Pirate

In The Blood Moon Pirate, Jack Aaron is at the zoo with his family on a day off from work. Incidentally, this is the same day that Luna Wells and Freckles are going to the zoo. After Freckles chases him through the zoo without Luna's knowledge, he is arrested by police officers. In an attempt to shoot them, Freckles blocks the bullet, which ricochets back at him and lands in his leg. Luna never had an idea Jack was at the zoo.

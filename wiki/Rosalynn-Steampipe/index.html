Summary

Cog is the main character of the Steam War series. She was a student on the Globetrotter when the Steam War began, with her friends Gogs, Sprocket, and Sail. She doesn't know much about her own life initially, but finds more out as the series progresses. Staunchly opposed to violence and a firm fighter for her friends, Cog heads up the Cogwheel Renegades in The Brass Dove.

Early Life

Not much is known about Cog's early life. She suffered a serious head injury from a hurricane when younger, and was flown to Dr. Mengele's Island, where experiments were done on her to mutate her genetics. From there, Agent Ike took her to the Globetrotter, where she became friends with Gogs and Sprocket.



The Steam War

During the events of The Steam War, Cog is going through the motions at school when she encounters two new potential friends: Lukas Uhrwehrk (das Gehirn) and Katie Bowler (Sail). From there, tensions rise as war looms, and she is kidnapped by Lukas and asked what she knows about the happenings of the world. She then encounters the Black Horizon on board the Mumba, and the crew defeats the invading Nazis.



The Brass Dove

In The Brass Dove, it is revealed that Cog is the daughter of Richard Steampipe (Amp), the now-deceased pilot of the Globetrotter. After a press conference, they are sent to Brass Isle, where they meet up with her old roommate Cal Valentine, who was on the Island with her. Cal's boyfriend Zac joins them on their quest to find and kill Hitler, which they end up succeeding in. In the meantime, Cog finds more out about her life from documents sent by Ike. It is revealed she is a genetic experiment, and had multiple experimental tests applied to her.

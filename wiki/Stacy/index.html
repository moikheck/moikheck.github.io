Summary

Stacy is a highschooler who was on a trip to Egypt with her classmates when their plane was taken down by a mysterious attack. She survived the initial crash, but remained in a safe area of the Hindenburg while her classmates went to find something to help her.

The Ghost of the Hindenburg

In The Ghost of the Hindenburg, Stacy made it to the Hindenburg with her classmates Tes Simms, Holly McCay, and Cody Smith. She spent most of the time in the ship hiding away in a safe area while the crew hunted for water.

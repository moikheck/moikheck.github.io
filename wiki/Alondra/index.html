Summary

Alondra is a girl who worked for the Marrow household in The Blood Moon Pirate before briefly joining Luna Wells on her adventures, remaining in the airship during Luna's hunts. She was accused of reporting their location to Orion Muldane, and then kicked out.

The Blood Moon Pirate

Alondra worked for the Marrow manor as a kitchen aid. After the Marrows were murdered, she joined Luna on her quest to hunt down more of Bijabers' crew.

Occasionally, Alondra would help Luna with some things around the airship, particularly when they got into a battle. Later, it is revealed she goes to a soup kitchen to help people while Luna is out hunting.

Alondra shows special attention to Luna and her escapades, leading Luna to believe she's been reporting their location to Orion and the Clockwork. After some trial and error, tensions boil over, and Luna removes her from the Constellation.

Summary

Luna Wells is the titular point-of-view main character from Luna on the Run. She and her best friend Freckles, a steambot cat, end up in trouble after stealing the airship of notorious crime boss Slaphand, forcing Luna on an epic quest to rescue Freckles. Along the way, she displays her twisted morals and internal struggles.

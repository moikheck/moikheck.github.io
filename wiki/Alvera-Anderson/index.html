Early Life

Alvera Anderson was a world traveler and entertainer that went by the name of "The Amazing Alvera Anderson." He claims to have created Aurora, which is likely a lie, as Aurora is a steambot. He was then kidnapped by Nazis during WWII, and forced to stay on the Hindenburg.



The Ghost of the Hindenburg

In The Ghost of the Hindenburg, Alvera offers Aurora to Tes Simms and Holly McCay after they visit his living quarters. He is the first time they visually see one of the phased.

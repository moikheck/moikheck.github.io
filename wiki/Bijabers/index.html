Summary

Bijabers is the head of a large crime syndicate that spans multiple countries, notably Italy, the United States of America, and Carmsborough. He has multiple underlings over the course of the Carmsborough Vigilantes series, and interacts with Luna Wells twice. He escapes his murder the second time they meet thanks to intervention from Orion and the Clockwork.

Early Life

Not much is known about Bijabers pre-Luna on the Run, but it is known that Agent Ike and S6 were hunting him down for a long while beforehand. He runs many illegal or barely legal businesses, including casinos, drug runs, housing companies, munitions companies, and more.



Luna on the Run

In Luna on the Run, interaction with Bijabers is limited to the moment on the docks after Luna had nearly escaped from Slaphand. There, he taunts her, earning his place on her list.



The Blood Moon Pirate

In The Blood Moon Pirate, Bijabers is stationed in one of his fake company buildings in New York City. Luna flies to the city from Carmsborough, fights her way up to get to him, and locks him in a cage before Orion and the Clockwork arrive. From there, the vigilantes battle, and he escapes her wrath.

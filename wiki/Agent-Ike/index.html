Summary

Dwight Wings, better known as Agent Ike, is an agent of the American covert intelligence agency S6. He seems to know more than he ever lets on, and is revealed to have been a time traveler in The Brass Dove, claiming something has changed in the past that makes the future uncertain.
Dwight appears in more entries than any other character, being in all Steam War books, all Carmsborough Vigilantes books, and The Ghost of the Hindenburg. Dwight is also married to Kate Wings, and has children.

Early Life

Not much is known about Ike's early life, but it is known that he works at S6 and hunts people like Bijabers, a crime syndicate boss.



The Steam War

Ike's appearances in The Steam War are mostly limited to memories of either Cog's or Cal's, where it's made clear he helped them both escape the awful scenarios that life found them in, particularly Dr. M's Island.



The Brass Dove

Ike plays a much more hands-on role in The Brass Dove than other entries, where he actively works in tandem with the Cogwheel Renegades to find and kill Hitler. Along the way, he trains the crew and gives them access to classified information about their pasts.



Luna on the Run

In Luna on the Run, Ike interacts with Luna Wells, a child vigilante seeking to hunt Slaphand down to save her cat Freckles. They have a strained relationship at first, but in the end, work together to enact her goals.



The Blood Moon Pirate

Ike's appearance in The Blood Moon Pirate is brief, centering solely on the murder of P-Man, where Luna breaks into the Carmsborough S6 headquarters. He may or may not have been in on Luna's attack.



Orion and the Clockwork

In Orion and the Clockwork, Ike appears with Agent Mike, his brother, and Kate, his wife. They are working to undermine Syndra's grasp on Carmsborough and recruit Orion to their mission.



The Ghost of the Hindenburg

In The Ghost of the Hindenburg, Ike appears only for a moment at the end, in the future after Tes Simms and Holly McCay escape with Aurora. He attempts to talk to them, but they are teleported away before any real talk can happen.

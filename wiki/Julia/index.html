Summary

Julia was a passenger of the Hindenburg when it crashed. She became one of the phased that survived, and was aliased "The Sobber" after spending most of her time crying.

  • Name: Julia
  • Aliases: The Sobber
  • Species: Human/Phased
  • Gender: Female
  • Sexuality: Straight
  • Appears in:
    • The Ghost of the Hindenburg

Early Life

Nothing is known about Julia's early life, except that she was on board the Hindenburg when it crashed.



The Ghost of the Hindenburg

In The Ghost of the Hindenburg, Julia ends up crying and screaming a few times in the ship, until Tes Simms and Holly McCay discover they can use Aurora to see her. Then, she helps them to find water and get to the top floor of the ship.

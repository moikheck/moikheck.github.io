Summary

The Smoker was a passenger of the Hindenburg when it crashed. They became one of the phased that survived, and was aliased "The Smoker" due to the clouds of smoke they leave behind.

The Ghost of the Hindenburg

In The Ghost of the Hindenburg, the Smoker antagonizes the survivors of the plane crash, and kills Cody Smith. He then attempts to get Tes Simms and Holly McCay as they run to the captain's quarters.

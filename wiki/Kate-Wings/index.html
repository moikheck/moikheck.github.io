Summary

Kate Wings is one of the original airship captains of the Steam War. She is married to Dwight Wings.

  • Name: Kate Wings
  • Aliases: Captain Wings
  • Species: Human
  • Gender: Female
  • Sexuality: Straight
  • Appears in:
    • The Steam War
    • Orion and the Clockwork

Early Life

Kate's early life showcases the fact that she snuck into an airship competition, disguised as her husband, and won. After revealing herself as a woman, there was backlash, but she still got to join the Steam War.



The Steam War

In The Steam War, Kate is captain of the Spigot and fights off the Nazi Blitz with the Nikola. This is shown as a flashback chapter.



Orion and the Clockwork

In Orion and the Clockwork, Kate joins her husband in a quest to save Carmsborough from Syndra by taking Orion to see the Gearmaster.

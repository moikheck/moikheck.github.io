Summary

Cleopatra is one of Bijabers' crime boss leaders. In Luna on the Run, she is responsible for shipping things out of Carmsborough, but fails before Luna Wells and Agent Ike arrive.

  • Aliases: None
  • Species: Human

Work for Bijabers

Cleopatra's role is not made explicitly clear in Luna on the Run, but it is known that she works for Bijabers.



Luna on the Run

In Luna on the Run, she is aiming to ship things out of Carmsborough with her airship, including Freckles, but is stopped when Luna and Agent Ike appear to rescue him.

Summary

Mary is a prisoner on Blood Moon Island that was unable to pay her debts. She aided Luna Wells in her scoping and attempted escape of the island, but passed during the start of the Steam War when crushed by the ceiling caving in.

Early Life

Before being a prisoner on Blood Moon Island, Mary had a normal life in Carmsborough, but barely made it from month to month. She was sent to the island after being unable to pay her debts to Slaphand.



Luna on the Run

In Luna on the Run, Mary helped Luna figure out how to best navigate the tumultuous island, and even helped her scout the administration building. After being found and captured by Slaphand, she was imprisoned with Luna in his office, until the start of the Steam War. After that, when trying to escape, a pile of rubble ended up crushing her, and Luna left her to die.

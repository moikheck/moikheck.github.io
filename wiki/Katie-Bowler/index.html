Summary

Katie Bowler, better known as Sail, is a member of the Cogwheel Renegades. She has heterochromia, and struggles with her sexuality.

  • Name: Katie Bowler
  • Aliases: Sail
  • Species: Human
  • Gender: Female
  • Sexuality: Bisexual
  • Appears in:
    • The Steam War
    • The Brass Dove

Early Life

Sail's early life was noted by her time with her brother, Hunter.



The Steam War

In The Steam War, Sail wins a pair of golden goggles, and Cog adds her to their friend group. She then gets into a relationship with Gogs.



The Brass Dove

In The Brass Dove, Sail is looking to avenge the death of Gogs by targeting Adolf Hitler. In the meantime, she struggles with her sexuality, as she realizes she's attracted to Cog.

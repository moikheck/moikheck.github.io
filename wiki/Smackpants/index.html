Summary

Smackpants is one of Bijabers' crime boss leaders and brother of Dancypants. He was target of Luna Wells and Freckles in The Blood Moon Pirate, and was killed in his self-titled chapter.

Work for Bijabers

Smackpants' role is not made explicitly clear in The Blood Moon Pirate, but it is known that he works for Bijabers. He has a base in Italy and in Carmsborough.



The Blood Moon Pirate

In The Blood Moon Pirate, Smackpants is fleeing to Italy on a ship when Luna and Freckles come to fight.

After Orion and the Clockwork arrive, Luna finds and kills him.

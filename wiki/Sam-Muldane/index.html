Summary

Sam Muldane is the younger sister of Orion Muldane. She lives with him and her dad in the Housing District of Carmsborough. Generally, she likes to antagonize her brother, but has no ill intentions.

Early Life

When Sam was young, her mother was diagnosed with cancer. Her and Orion would rest on the footsteps of their apartment complex together waiting for their mom and dad to get back from the doctor's. Not much else is known about Sam's early life.



Orion and the Clockwork

In Orion and the Clockwork, we find out that Sam likes to tease her older brother whenever she gets a chance. Orion views this as sibling rivalry.

Early Life

Not much is known about Sprocket's early life, but it is revealed that he lived alone with his dad after he and his mom separated. Once an opportunity appeared on the Globetrotter, he left his dad and has had a strained relationship ever since.



The Steam War

In The Steam War, we are introduced to Sprocket and his dynamic in the friend group. As one of the first people to meet Cog, he and Gogs quickly became her best friends. Sprocket tends to be more of a reserved, careful, and thoughtful person, opting to protect his friends or maintain the peace as often as possible.

When Lukas, the New German exchange student, reveals his love for Cog, Sprocket is threatened, as he does not trust Lukas, and also has strong feelings for Cog. He is also the one to suspect that something is wrong with Gogs, although does not receive confirmation about that until later.



The Brass Dove

During the events of The Brass Dove, Sprocket helms his ship the Starjumper, acting as pilot and mediator for many situations. While reluctantly coming to terms with the fact that everyone will always prefer to go with the more dangerous option, Sprocket must also come to terms with the fact that Cog does not see him as a romantic option, but as a best friend. Ultimately, he prefers that, afraid of destroying their friendship.



The Cogwheel Renegades

Sprocket is set to make an appearance in The Cogwheel Renegades, the third installment in the Steam War series.

Summary

Tes is a mute highschooler who was on a trip to Egypt with her classmates when their plane was taken down by a mysterious attack. She is the forced to survive through the wreckage of the Hindenburg, which had previously been missing. From there, she is sent into different universes after finding a steambot rabbit named Aurora with Holly McCay, on a quest to make it back home, while developing feelings for her companion.

Early Life

It is unknown what exactly happened in Tes' early life, but is is known that her dad went to jail, and her mother is dead. In turn, she's lived with her grandparents. Tes was named after Nikola Tesla.



The Ghost of the Hindenburg

In The Ghost of the Hindenburg, Tes survives a plane crash that lands her and her classmates at the wreckage of the Hindenburg. She attempts to survive the haunted interior of the lost airship along with her classmates Holly McCay, Stacy, Cody, and Wesley. Once only Holly and Tes remain, they find Aurora, and make their all the way to the phased captain of the ship, Max Pruss. When something goes wrong at the end of the story, we hear Tes speak for the first time.



The Ruins of Alpha Centauri

In The Ruins of Alpha Centauri, Tes and Holly encounter Sola Hoku, a survivor of the Alpha Centauri Invasion. They help Tes and Holly, taking the two to the elders, and providing housing in Omega Centauri. After some experimentation requests on Aurora by the Omega Centauri scientists, as well as some close calls with the human-hunting galvaknights, they fled Omega Centauri, avoided death by the galvanaught, and fled to another universe without Sola.

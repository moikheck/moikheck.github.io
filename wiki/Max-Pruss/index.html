Summary

Max Pruss was captain of the Hindenburg when it crashed. He became one of the phased that survived. He is the main antagonist of The Ghost of the Hindenburg.

  • Aliases: The Captain

Early Life

Max Pruss was a captain for the Nazi army in WWII and The Steam War. He was piloting the Hindenburg when it crashed in the Sahara, and was warped in spacetime in the process.



The Ghost of the Hindenburg

In The Ghost of the Hindenburg, Pruss welcomes Tes Simms and Holly McCay into his quarters before revealing he attacked their plane. He then disintegrates after coming into contact with an orb of spirit energy.

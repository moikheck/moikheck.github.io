Summary

P-Man is one of Bijabers' crime boss leaders. He was target of Luna Wells and Freckles in The Blood Moon Pirate, and was killed in his self-titled chapter.

Work for Bijabers

P-Man's role was to be a mole for Bijabers in the organization S6. He worked to undermine their attempts at getting close to Bijabers.



The Blood Moon Pirate

In The Blood Moon Pirate, P-Man was killed by Luna in the S6 facility in Carmsborough.

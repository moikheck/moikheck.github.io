Michael Heckman's

THE BRASS DOVE

"War is not a man's sport. War is no sport at all."

After a surprise reunion with her former roommate, Cog and her crew follow the directions of Agent Ike, who urges them to join the war effort as a scout ship. Tension starts to boil between the friendgroup, however, when everyone wants something different, and Cog can't figure out who she is or what she's supposed to be.

About the Author

Michael has always had a love for writing that stems from writing a short story about turtles on his family computer in second grade. From there, he never stopped writing, and wrote his first ten-thousand-word book in the third grade, igniting his passion for storytelling.

Now, the only thing stopping Michael from writing more is his schedule. Ideas like Luna on the Run and The Brass Dove keep him up at night, plotting his creative path forward.

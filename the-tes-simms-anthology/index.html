The Tes Simms Anthology

Tes had prepared for the plane crash. What she hadn't prepared for was the game of survival afterward.

Stranded in the desert, she and her remaining classmates are seeking shelter from the harsh climate when they come across the battered remains of the Hindenburg, an airship that had been lost for nearly two decades. The group takes refuge inside, but when the disembodied sound of sobbing fills the halls, they start to wonder if the haunted ship should've remained lost.

After escaping the haunted shell of the Hindenburg, Tes, Holly, and Aurora find themselves in the middle of a scorched plains on a different planet three-thousand years in the future. With the aid of Sola, one of the underground survivors in Omega Centauri, they hope to make it back home while avoiding the grasp of the galvaknights: sound- and heat-detecting robots ready to tear them limb from limb.

Having avoided an untimely demise via the Galvanaught, Tes, Holly, and Aurora find themselves back on Earth. Only this time, they're one thousand years in the future, and everyone seems to have superpowers. When loyalty lines are drawn between the Necromancers, a group of people with forbidden auras who have taken control of the country, and a resistance team trying to take them down headed by an alternate universe Luna Wells, Tes and Holly must take risks to find their way back to their own universe, while exploring their complicated feelings for each other.

Carrying their newfound auras from the futuristic world of superpowered people, Tes, Holly, and Aurora come crashing into Sola again, who has found refuge hiding within the ranks of a cult. A cult which intends to end the world as soon as the Apollo 11 rocket launches toward the moon. The group must challenge the cult leader, Amar, and bring an end to the dangers that threaten to come from a shadowy, unknowable place, or the world will plummet into eternal darkness. Oh, and Tes and Holly are finally honest about their feelings for each other.

Reintroducing Patreon

September 24th, 2025

I've always had a dream of making my writing a sustainable habit. On top of all the other things happening in life, being able to write is dependent on the circumstances. There are weeks that go by where I have the time and resources I need to work, and there are dry spells where I can't touch it for a month. This was something I tried to tackle in the past, but was ultimately unprepared for. In hopes of providing more consistency for myself and you, I have an idea that will benefit both parties!


Introducing my new Patreon account. Here, you can support me by becoming a monthly patron while getting some perks for it. For example, I will be having exclusive content in the Patreon, like my upcoming story, The Haunting of Hathaway Manor.


I'm also launching my Patreon with early access to my currently publishing story, Imposters in Cult Clothing. After this, Access to chapters of the next Tes Simms Anthology entry will be available in October.


Interested? Take a look at my Patreon today and see if it looks like a fit for you!

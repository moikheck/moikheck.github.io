Luna on the Run - Announcement

January 31st, 2022

"It's hard for me to remember a time before I had to resort to stealing.

Well, that’s not entirely true. I remember the begging phase, too. Begging for food, money, a bed to sleep on, and to be accepted by an orphanage. Ever since mom died and our landlord kicked me out for not being able to pay, that’s all I’ve ever been able to do. But begging never worked, and, unsurprisingly, I switched to stealing.

Is it moral?

Is forcing a kid to live on the street for their entire teenage life because their only living parent dies moral?

I didn't think so."

Follow Luna, a homeless teen with skewed morals, and her mechanical cat Freckles in their struggles to live a normal, problem-free life that keeps getting interrupted by bad men with bad ideas.

LUNA ON THE RUN is a completely free web-based book focusing on the duo as they try to survive on the streets of Carmsborough. Chapters 1 & 2 will release on February 25th, and every chapter after will be released on the Friday of each week (as long as everything goes well).


Get ready for LUNA ON THE RUN, coming soon!
